Our Signature Dish

Shan Noodles

Khao Swe Thoke

Our most beloved dish, Shan Noodles originated from the Shan State in eastern Myanmar. This iconic dish represents the perfect harmony of flavors and textures.

What makes it special:

  • Fresh rice noodles made daily
  • Marinated chicken or pork
  • Roasted peanuts for crunch
  • Fresh herbs and pickled vegetables
  • Savory-sweet sauce blend

Bringing Myanmar's Pride to Japan

Established with a passion for sharing authentic Myanmar cuisine, The Golden Star is a culinary bridge connecting two wonderful cultures. Our mission is to introduce the rich, vibrant, and comforting flavors of our homeland to the people of Japan.

We believe that food is a powerful way to tell a story, and every dish on our menu carries a piece of our heritage—from the bustling markets of Yangon to the serene beauty of Inle Lake and the mountainous Shan State.

The Heart of Our Kitchen

Our specialty, Shan Noodles, embodies everything we stand for: tradition, quality, and authentic taste. This dish has been perfected over generations in Myanmar's Shan State, and we've brought that same tradition to Tokyo.

Every morning, our chefs prepare fresh rice noodles using traditional methods. The marinated meats are seasoned with a secret family recipe, and the sauce is balanced to achieve the perfect combination of savory, sweet, and tangy notes.

Whether you choose it dry (mixed) or in soup, our Shan Noodles delivers an authentic taste experience that transports you straight to Myanmar.

Our Commitment to Quality

We are dedicated to maintaining authenticity while using locally sourced ingredients whenever possible. Beyond our famous Shan Noodles, our menu features other traditional favorites like Mohinga, Laphet Thoke, and various home-style curries.

Every ingredient is carefully selected, and each recipe follows traditional preparation methods passed down through generations. Our chefs take pride in crafting meals that are both delicious and true to the spirit of Myanmar home-cooking.

A Place for Everyone

Whether you're from Myanmar and missing the taste of home, or you're Japanese and curious about a new cuisine, or you're simply a food lover exploring Asian flavors—our restaurant welcomes you with open arms.

We've created a warm, inviting space where cultural exchange happens naturally over shared meals. Many of our regular customers come specifically for our Shan Noodles, and we love seeing their faces light up with every bite.
